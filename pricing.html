to attend.

What's included Ce qui est inclus

Everything
you need.

Your ticket covers the full experience, facilitation, space, and park entry. Bring your own food and camping gear.

  • 5 workshops with experienced CI facilitators
  • 3 contact jams (evening & morning)
  • Campfire nights with music & song circles
  • Group waterfront campsite for the full 4 days
  • La Mauricie National Park entry fee
  • BYO: personal food & drink
  • BYO: camping gear (tent, sleeping bag, pad)
